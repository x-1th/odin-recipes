Simple Turkey Chili

Description

This turkey chili recipe is very simple but hearty with plenty of flavors—it tastes even better on the second day! I serve this chili with low-fat Cheddar cheese and low-fat sour cream. It's also delicious with crackers or cornbread.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (16-ounce) can kidney beans - drained, rinsed, and mashed
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon paprika or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add turkey; cook and stir until evenly browned, 6 to 8 minutes. Stir in onion and cook until softened.
  3. Add water; stir in tomatoes, kidney beans, and garlic.
  4. Stir in chili powder, paprika, oregano, cayenne pepper, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  5. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer, covered, until flavors blend and chili thickens slightly, about 30 minutes.
